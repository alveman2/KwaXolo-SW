# Category C — Digital Marketing & Sales

---

## C1: Setting up a WhatsApp Business profile

**Student task type:** Step-by-step guided task  
**Interaction pattern:** Student sets up a real WhatsApp Business profile during the task

**Local grounding hooks:**
- WhatsApp Business is free and runs on any Android phone — near-universal in the community
- Hlobisile Pearl Studios uses WhatsApp Business for photography booking
- Catalogue feature: students can list products with photos and prices

**Hard rules:**
- Use WhatsApp Business (the separate app), not regular WhatsApp
- Include exact steps: Play Store → search "WhatsApp Business" → Install → set up with phone number
- Explain the difference between a personal and a business profile in one sentence
- Student task must result in a live WhatsApp Business profile with at least a name and photo

## C2: Creating a Facebook page for your business

**Student task type:** Step-by-step guided task  
**Interaction pattern:** Student creates a real Facebook page during the task

**Local grounding hooks:**
- Facebook (Meta) is used by adults in the community for buying and selling — Facebook Marketplace is active
- Inkify promotes services via Facebook
- A Facebook Page is separate from a personal profile — explain this clearly

**Hard rules:**
- Use the Facebook mobile app, not the desktop site
- Student task must produce a live page with a name, profile photo, and one post
- Warn about privacy: the student should not put their home address on the page
- Acknowledge slow loading on mobile data — WiFi is recommended

## C3: Taking good product photos with a phone

**Student task type:** Try this now  
**Interaction pattern:** Student takes 3 photos of a real object and compares them

**Local grounding hooks:**
- Hlobisile Pearl Studios (photography) as an example of professional image standards
- Natural light is free and available — no equipment needed
- Background: plain wall, cloth, or ground (not a cluttered table)

**Hard rules:**
- Student takes real photos during the task — not hypothetical
- Three shots required: one bad (no guidance), one better (with guidance), one good (after all tips)
- Tips must be usable without any equipment: no tripod, no lights, no editing apps required
- End with how to share the photo on WhatsApp or Facebook

## C4: Writing a sales message that gets replies

**Student task type:** Write and reflect  
**Interaction pattern:** Student writes a real WhatsApp sales message and (optionally) sends it

**Local grounding hooks:**
- WhatsApp as the primary sales channel — most community commerce happens here
- Spaza shop WhatsApp groups as existing reference for local buying and selling
- Frame the message as personal and specific — not a broadcast

**Hard rules:**
- Provide a message template with 4 parts: what you are selling / why it is good / price / how to order
- Student writes their own version of the template — not a copy-paste
- The reflection question: did you send the message? What happened (or what do you expect will happen)?
- Never recommend sending unsolicited messages to strangers — only to people the student knows

## C5: Using Facebook Marketplace to sell locally

**Student task type:** Explore and observe  
**Interaction pattern:** Student browses Facebook Marketplace and then lists one item

**Local grounding hooks:**
- Facebook Marketplace is active in KwaZulu-Natal for local buying and selling
- Zero cost to list — no fees, no delivery required (buyer collects)
- Inkify has listed services in the area

**Hard rules:**
- First step: browse the local marketplace and observe what is selling
- Second step: create one listing (can be a product or service)
- Listing must include: photo, title, price, and a contact method (WhatsApp number)
- Warn: never share personal home address in a public listing
