## TASK TITLE
Set Up Your Professional Email

## WHAT YOU WILL DO
You will create a Gmail account using your real name and send your first professional email.

## STEPS

**Step 1 of 5 — Check if Gmail is on your phone**  
Open your phone and look for the Gmail app. It looks like a coloured "M" on a white background. If you do not have it, open the Play Store, search for "Gmail", and tap Install. It is free.

**Step 2 of 5 — Create your account**  
Open Gmail. Tap "Create account", then tap "For myself". Fill in your first name and last name — use your real name, not a nickname. Tap Next.  
*Tip: If you already have a Google account, you can skip this step and use the account you have.*

**Step 3 of 5 — Choose your email address**  
Gmail will suggest an email address based on your name. If it includes numbers you do not like, you can choose "Create your own Gmail address" and type a clean version — for example, `thandi.nzama@gmail.com`. Tap Next and follow the steps to set a password.  
*Tip: Write your email address and password somewhere safe — you will need them again.*

**Step 4 of 5 — Write a professional email**  
You are now in your inbox. Tap the pencil icon (bottom right) to write a new email. In the "To" field, type your own email address — you will send this to yourself as a practice.  
- Subject: Practice email  
- Message:  
  > Dear [your name],  
  > I am testing my new email address. My name is [your name] and I am learning how to use email for my business.  
  > Kind regards,  
  > [your name]  

Tap the arrow (Send) button.

**Step 5 of 5 — Check your sent email**  
Tap the three lines (menu) on the top left. Find "Sent". You should see the email you just sent. Tap it to open and read it.  
*If you do not see it, wait 1 minute and check again — sometimes it takes a moment.*

## THINK ABOUT THIS
Write your answer in the box below:

*You now have a professional email address. Think of one real situation in your life — a job, a customer, a school — where you might use this email address in the next few months. Describe the situation and what you would say in the email.*

## TIME
Estimated time: 12–15 minutes
